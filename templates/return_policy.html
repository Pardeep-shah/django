Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you’re not happy, we’re here to help.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Items must be returned within 7–30 days of purchase.
  • Items must be unused, in the same condition as received, and in the original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase is required (invoice or order confirmation).

Non-Returnable Items

Items such as downloadable software, gift cards, or personal care products are not eligible for return.

Refund Process

Once we receive your item, we’ll inspect it and notify you of the status of your refund. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our return policy, contact our support team at support@example.com.
